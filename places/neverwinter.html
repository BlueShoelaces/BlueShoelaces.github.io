Overview (from canon)

Thriving, diverse city decimated by volcano eruption. Now there's a rift. Neverwinter river heats the city. Fire elementals living in Mt Hotenow. City is being restored to its former glory.

Neverwinter Academy

Founded by Ilanis Firahel about ten years ago. She and her student, Wickabree, are hoping their research will help put Neverwinter back on the map.
