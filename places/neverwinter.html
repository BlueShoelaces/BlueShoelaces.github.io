Timeline of events

1451Mount Hotenow erupts! Lays waste to city, kills ruling royal family, creating Chasm. Shortly afterward, surviving citizens build the wall.
1467Dagult Neverember declares himself “Lord Protector” (and rightful heir). Starts the New Neverwinter movement.
1474Sons of Alagondar form and publicly oppose Lord Neverember.
1479By now, Neverwinter Guard is composed mostly of Mintarn mercenaries, as well as some local militia groups.
1485Chasm is magically sealed, at great financial cost. Wall is torn down.
1486Ilanis Firahel establishes Neverwinter Academy
1489Neverember exiled from Waterdeep, becomes full-time Lord Protector of Neverwinter. Sons of Alagondar chill out.
1491Present day.

Overview (from canon)

Thriving, diverse city decimated by volcano eruption. Now there's a rift. Neverwinter river heats the city. Fire elementals living in Mt Hotenow. City is being restored to its former glory.

Neverwinter Academy

Founded by Ilanis Firahel about five years ago. She and her student, Wickabree, are hoping their research will help put Neverwinter back on the map.
